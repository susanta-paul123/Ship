Mulitple benefits stem from the use of a single contact aggregator.

  • Set and Forget. Confidence in delivery once confirmed.

  • Readily avaliable POD and audit trial.
  • Single itemised invoice of mutliple carriers.
  • Once contact number for support and assistance.

Best Carrier for the Job

Based on your customised requirements and preferecnes, Shiip will select carriers, pricing nd service levels for each job on a case by case basis. Taking into account specific or regualtory requirements, each job will be assigned only to carriers that have met your budget or unique handling specifications. Don’t lose valuable time with vehicles, drivers or carrier not suitable for yoru needs, and always ensure only qualified, trained or certified carriers are assigned to fulfill your deliveries.

The Tech

Shiip is the first Fullfillment as a Service (FaaS) where clients are not required to subscribe to a Platform (PaaS) or Software (SaaS) to access the variety of Same Day Delivery carriers. Whether Shiip is integrated via API or used manually through an adhoc online login, the convenience and benefits of the technology quickly add up to a seemless, scalable and powerful resource to enhance your Last Mile delivery service and put your brand on par with the world leading e-tailers and eComm pioneers.

  • One Integration, once.
  • Algorithm is always leveraging the buying power of 1 platform accessing mutliple carriers on your behalf.
  • Obligation free – no penalties or fees for labels, not ordering, over ordering or out of spec parcels.
  • Always be using the latest, most secure and upto date technology, carriers, services and rates avaliable. No need to install or upgrade software, assign dedicated personell to monitor and maintain system or invest in building your own tools.
