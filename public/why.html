What can Shiip do for you?

Creating a positve online customer experience is only complete when online orders and fulfilled in a speedy, adaptive and reliable manner, worthy of regular and repeat purchases. Shiip provides businesses wanting to leverage the growth of online ordering access to the best carriers, broadest geographical coverage and a widest range of delivery service levels to ensure your customers can enjoy their purchses as quickly as possible within cost and fulfillment parameters. Shiip is the only truly adaptive and responsive FaaS (Fulfillment as a Service) able to scale to your requirements whilst responding to external conditions and competitive pressures. We bundle the planning, sourcing and assignment of the best delivery services for your needs in a single fulfillment solution. Shiip keeps your Same Day fulfillment solution competitive, adaptive and scalable.
