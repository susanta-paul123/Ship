01

Upload data to Shiip – A Standardised format for receiving and processing your delivery data

  • Simplistic
  • Easy to navigate
  • Quick/Timely
  • Ad hoc and API data – covers more access
  • capabilities and formats.

02

Best Price - Service

  • Timely – Easy to compare pricing and service differences between vendors
  • Adaptable and customisable for your needs / regulatory requirements.
  • Leveraging the aggregation model allows smaller and sporadic vendors to access better pricing, service levels and response rates.
  • Screening - Able to customise your service requirements from the onset.

03

Confirmed Delivery ETA = Fulfillment with confidence

  • Confidence and confirmation that YOUR customer will get the delivery as You have promised.
  • Guaranteed delivery timeframe is now on offer as a standard service from your business.

04

Packaging and Label

  • Quality made, fit for purpose and competitively priced packaging - satchels and boxes available.
  • Auto generated labels – free and unlimited.
  • Access to QR code generator for cross selling marketing opportunities to end receivers to scan QR and redeem special offers at the time of delivery.
  • Recycled, Eco Friendly and sustainable.

05

Live Job Status on Platform

  • Delivery status visibility and access to all parties.
  • Self manageable and quick response for your customers’ enquiries. 24/7 from anywhere I the world.

06

Picked up from client outlet / DC or 3PL.

  • Assigned to a Driver with unique consignment number
  • Security check/protocol undertaken to ensure the right driver is picking up the right parcel every time. Ensures the right delivery to the right customer.
  • Allows for customisation for the nearest and most cost effective pick up location to the customer delivery destination.

07

SMS notification to receiver

  • Confirmation messages to receivers with accurate delivery details.
  • Gives your customers a link to live tracking and status updates.
  • Enhances the customer experience and confidence in your business.

08

Optimised Delivery Route and live tracking

  • Ensure the delivery expectations and timeframes meet the actual and current traffic conditions.
  • Provides your customers with reasonable and realistic expectations. Deliver on YOUR promise.
  • Adaptable routes and delivery ETA reflective of live weather and traffic conditions. Keeping customers informed - no surprises or delays.

09

Live Edit of Delivery Instructions

  • Flexibility and adaptability in the delivery service reflecting customers last minute changes.

10

Contact Driver

  • Direct access to communicate with drivers to leave specific hand over instructions. Ensure deliveries are not lost, returned, re-directed or wrongly handed over unless the customer has instructed and approved the change.
  • Put your customer in control of the process. Improve rates and likelihood of successful deliveries - All but remove returned and lost items.
  • Removes the need to go through Customer Service or another third party to re-convey instructions.

11

Deliver to Customer Direct

  • Brings brands closer to their customers by overcoming distance.
  • Immediate Delivery is the new retail. Presents more opportunities to win the
  • customer online ahead of competitors.
  • Online customers expect immediate fulfillment as part of any online sale.

12

SMS POD Sent to Sender and Receiver

  • Closed loop process End to End tracking and audit trial.
  • 24-hour access to check and verify claims of failed deliveries from customers.
  • All records archived and accessible via login.

13

Single invoice for all carriers

  • Multiple accounts with multiple carriers on one account.
  • Consolidates your delivery costs into a single cost centre.
  • Saves time and costs in accounting and credit claims.
  • Ensures clients and customers are always getting the best deals and service from carriers through the single aggregator’s platform.
  • 24/7 access to reconcile accounts.
